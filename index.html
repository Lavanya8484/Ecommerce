This is our love for natural chocolate.

Vores venner i Afrika

In a greenhouse near a cocoa plantation close to San Pédro in Ivory Coast, a group of local women are cultivating vegetables and fruit. It is not possible to grow vegetables and fruit in this area due to soil conditions, lack of water, and the heat. However, with our greenhouse, the right soil, and a water supply, we have contributed to a nourishing future for our cocoa farmers and their families.
